Drone Technology Simplified:
From Purchase to Takeoff

About the Course

Imagine… a world with a drone in every home.

Drone Technology Simplified: From Purchase to Takeoff is a comprehensive introductory course taking you behind the hype and fanfare of drones—an industry growing like wildfire—to teach you how to buy, build, and fly your very first DIY drone (multicopter).

Ready to fly drones are great, however, if you want true performance and adaptability at a reasonable price, you’ll need to learn how to build your own aerial platform. But it can be hard when you’re first starting out to know which parts to buy and how to configure all of the components.

Drone pilots are earning up to $15,000 a day flying for Hollywood production companies, large utility companies, and government agencies. But before you can tap that source of wealth, you need to fully understand what makes a great craft and how it works.

You’ll be led by Arland Whitfield, founder of SkyworksPro, LLC, a full-service drone company. He’ll walk you through the very same decisions and methods he used to build his first drone.

Arland will be joined throughout the course by expert videographers, photographers, drone entrepreneurs, and lawyers to help you better appreciate and utilize your first drone.

This course is intended for the drone apprentice who wants to take their knowledge from 0 to 100 in only a short period of time—6 hours in fact. The best students will be ready to buy a full-scale craft and to search for new opportunities.

Course Objectives

We want to make sure you have all of the information and necessary resources to get started in the drone industry today. Our goal is to provide you a comprehensive background in the theory, purchasing, building, and operations of your very own drone. We’ve broken the course out into five distinct phases which dynamically build upon each other.

We won’t make you remember formulas and schematics, however, you will learn the basic science and operational theory behind multirotor flight. We believe this is a very important topic many drone courses fail to adequately, if at all, teach.

A solid theoretical foundation of drone components and flight mechanics will help you better understand every purchase decision made throughout the life of your drone.

It’s easy to get overwhelmed by the hundreds of pages of motors, rotors, frames, and batteries you could conceivably buy for your drone. We’ve been there—lost, confused, and needing help.

We’ll expose you to some of the most reputable manufacturers and distributors in this section. We’ve also hooked up some pretty sweet deals with our distribution partners to make your drone build more affordable.

This section will dive deep into the specific techniques and how-to steps for assembling your drone. You’ll learn important skills such as soldering, electrical wiring, and equipment mounting.

Your drone will come alive in front of your eyes. By the end of the section you’ll have a beautiful craft which you can show off to all of your friends.

Hardware isn’t the only thing we’ll teach you to build. We’ll help you program your radio and flight controllers so you can easily and confidently fly your craft in any situation.

We made the entire course just for this section. All of the hard work you put in before this point will pay off the moment you hear those motors startup and see your drone liftoff. It’ll be like saying hello to your new baby.

We want to make sure you’re fully prepared for a safe and fun maiden voyage with your drone. So we’ll cover everything from preflight checks to various flying techniques. We’ll also throw in some advanced tips and tricks we’ve picked up along the way—making the whole experience that much more enjoyable.

If only it were as easy as turning the thing on and hitting the go switch. Unfortunately though, you’re bound to run into various in-flight and ground control problems needing a quick fix.

You’ll get exposed to some of the more common problems in this section and learn how to efficiently troubleshoot them. While we can’t cover everything under the sun in our videos, we’ll do our best.

We’re also implementing a great synchronous method for you to get your problems solved. We’ve decided to give you the opportunity to ask your most pressing questions in a weekly Hangout session with Arland.

We’re putting a cherry on top just for you! We’ve interviewed some of the best the drone industry has to offer. You’ll find great interviews and Q&A sessions in this section.
